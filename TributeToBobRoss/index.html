Robert Norman 'Bob' Ross

(10/29/1942 - 7/4/1995)

Bob Ross was a painter, television host, author, and instructor. Reruns of his show, 'The Joy of Painting' are still broadcast on cable television and Netflix.

Here is a timeline of Bob Ross's life:

  • 1942: Born in Daytona Beach, Florida.
  • 1961: Joins the United States Air Force.
  • 1961-81: Develops the 'Bob Ross' method of quick-painting.
  • 1981: Retires from the Air Force.
  • 1983: The first episode of 'The Joy of Painting' is broadcast.
  • 1989: Appears on 'The Joan Rivers Show'.
  • 1994: Appears on 'The Phil Donahue Show'.
  • 1994: The last episode of 'The Joy of Painting' airs.
  • 1995: Dies of lymphoma.
